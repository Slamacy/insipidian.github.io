Original Robot Design

This was the original Robot Helper design I created for my Final Year Project - "The Thinking City". Similar to the final design, this robot had become corrupted by a rogue A.I. and roamed the game space, threatening the player.

I designed this robot with general purpose and free movement in mind, and once made malevolent for it to act in an animalistic fashion. These choices, once executed, were deemed not threatening enough so we decided to scrap all work on this design and start again.
